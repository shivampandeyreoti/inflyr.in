The Problem

CREATOR MARKETING
IN INDIA IS BROKEN.

Brands spend without knowing what worked. Creators post without knowing what they'll earn. The whole system is built on hope — not data.

01
No Attribution

You pay a creator ₹50,000 and get a reel with 200K views. You have no idea if a single person bought. ROAS on creator spend is a guess — and that's not good enough.

02
Creative Fatigue

You recycle the same 3 creators. Same angle. Same hook. Your Meta ad account has seen it all. CPMs climb, ROAS drops, and the team runs out of content ideas every 6 weeks.

03
No System

DMs, spreadsheets, random WhatsApp groups — that's the workflow. Brief sent late. Content delivered wrong. Payment disputed. Same chaos every single campaign.

INFLYR respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect through inflyr.in and how we use it.
